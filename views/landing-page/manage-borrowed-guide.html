Manage Borrowed Books

Understand how to return, renew, or manage fines for your borrowed books

View Borrowed Books

Login and go to My Borrowed Books to view your currently issued books and due dates.

Returning Books

  1. Visit the library physically before the due date.
  2. The librarian searches using your Student ID.
  3. They click the “Return” button in the portal to mark it returned.

Renewing Books

  1. Ask the librarian for renewal before the due date.
  2. You can renew a book up to 5 times.
  3. The librarian updates the system to reflect the new due date.

Late Fee & Penalty

If you fail to return a book on time:

  • A late fee of ₹1 per day will be applied.
  • Continued delays may affect your borrowing privileges.
  • Make sure to check due dates regularly to avoid penalties.
