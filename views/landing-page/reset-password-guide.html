Reset Password

Securely reset your EduLibrary account password

Steps to Reset Password

  1. Go to the login page and click “Forgot Password?”.
  2. Enter your registered email or Student ID.
  3. Check your inbox for the reset link.
  4. Follow the link to set a new password.
  5. If you don't receive the email, contact the librarian or mail admin@edulibrary.com.
