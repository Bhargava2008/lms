Borrowing & Returning Books

Understand how to borrow and return books at EduLibrary

📘 How to Borrow a Book

  1. Search for the book in the catalog section.
  2. Click Request to Borrow.
  3. The librarian will approve your request.
  4. Once approved, the book is officially issued to you.

🔁 How to Return a Book

  1. Visit the library in person with the physical book.
  2. The librarian will verify your Student ID and books issued.
  3. They will click Return in their system to update the status.
  4. You're done — no student-side action needed.

📝 Renewal Policy

  • You may renew any book before the due date.
  • Ask the librarian or use the online system (if enabled).
  • You can renew a book up to 5 times.

⚠️ Borrowing Rules

  • Max Books: 3
  • Due Date: Return within 15 days
  • Late Fee: ₹1/day after due date

For any return-related queries, please visit the library help desk or contact us at library@college.edu
