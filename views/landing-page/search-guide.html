How to Search for Books

Quick steps to locate books in the EduLibrary system

Steps to Search

  1. Navigate to the Search or Catalog section of the dashboard.
  2. Enter keywords like the book title, author, or subject in the search bar.
  3. Use filters to narrow results (e.g., availability, category, department).
  4. Click on the result to view book details including availability and location.

If you're unable to find the book you're looking for, please contact the admin for assistance.
