Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our library system

In how many days should a book be returned once issued?

Books should be returned within 15 days from the date of issue.

If I fail to submit a book on time, what is the fine/penalty?

A fine of ₹1 per day will be charged for late returns.

How many books can be issued to a student at a time?

A maximum of 3 books can be issued at a time.

How many times can we renew a book?

You can renew a book up to 5 times.
