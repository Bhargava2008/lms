Hindu College of Engineering & Technology

Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, India

Established in 2011, HCET is a private engineering college affiliated with JNTU Kakinada, approved by AICTE and APSBTET. It is run by the Hindu College Committee, founded in 1934. The institution aims to nurture skilled engineers in emerging fields like AI, ML, & Robotics.

  • To foster academic excellence and innovation.
  • Provide quality education with industry collaboration.
  • Promote research, learning & ethical values.
  • AICTE Approved & Affiliated to JNTUK.
  • UG Engineering and Diploma Programs.
  • Modern labs, digital library, auditorium, hostel, cafeterias.
  • Active placement cell with campus recruitment opportunities.
